Fair Lab

A free app. To help you buy the most environmentally friendly products.

About us

Fair trade first.

Fair app is a free app created by group of students to spread awereness on how products are made. Our online webshop gives people the ability to buy products from fair trade handlers.

Green environment

One of our most important values is to ensure the products are made in a sustainable way.

Fair payment

Another value is that everyone involved has been payed and treated fairly.

Good working conditions

Its important to us that the people have the facility's they need.

No forced work

We do not approve child workers or forced workers. Everyone that works at the company needs to be registered.


Fair label

Company's can purchase our Fair Label. They need to proof that they match our requirements. Once they do they will be advertised as replacement products for those that don't.
